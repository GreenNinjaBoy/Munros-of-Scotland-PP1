Why Join Us?


The Munro Baggers were formed in 2022 shortly after the Covid 19 pandemic restrictions were lifted in Scotland. Our aim to bring together both beginners and experienced walkers who are looking for the challenge of conquering some of the biggest mountains in scotland.


Walking has many benifits such as: increased cardiovascular and pulmonary (heart and lung) fitness. reduced risk of heart disease and stroke. improved management of conditions such as hypertension (high blood pressure), high cholesterol, joint and muscular pain or stiffness, and diabetes. Walking has also been proven to improve mental health.


Being part of the Munro Baggers you will gain access to any planned walks, meetings and a chance to meet like minded people who have the drive and motivaton to 'bag' a Munro.

Munro History


The Munros are mountains within Scotland with an elevation of over 3000 feet (914 meters), and you can 'bag' a Munro by reaching the summit. The offical list of these mountains is a staggering 282 peaks across Scotland
